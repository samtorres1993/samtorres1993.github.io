Supreme Leader Ren Poster:

For my final I decided to make a political/propaganda inspired Star Wars poster.

The image of Kylo Ren was posterized in Photoshop and then shaded in using Illustrator.

The First Order symbol was created solely in Illustrator.

The text was added in Illustrator, the font is Aurebesh which is the alphabet used in the Star Wasrs universe.

The text reads, "Supreme Leader Ren" on top and "Join Me and We Can Rule Together" on the bottom.
